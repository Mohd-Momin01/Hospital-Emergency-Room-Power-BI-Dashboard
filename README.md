# 📌 Project Overview

Emergency Rooms generate thousands of patient records every month. Without a centralized reporting system, hospital administrators struggle to monitor operational performance and identify bottlenecks.

This project demonstrates how Power BI can transform raw hospital data into an interactive dashboard that enables faster, data-driven decision making.

The dashboard helps answer critical business questions such as:

- How many patients visited the Emergency Room?
- What is the average patient waiting time?
- Are patients being treated within the 30-minute operational target?
- Which departments receive the highest referrals?
- Which age groups visit most frequently?
- What are the busiest days and hours?
- How can staffing decisions be improved?

# 📈 Key Business Insights

### ⏳ Waiting Time

- Average Waiting Time: **35.3 Minutes**
- Only **40.68%** of patients were treated within the 30-minute target.

---

### 📅 Peak Load

Highest patient volume occurs on:

- Saturday
- Tuesday

Peak admission hours:

- 11 AM
- 7 PM

---

### 🏥 Department Referrals

Most referrals are made to:

- General Practice
- Orthopedics
- Physiotherapy

---

### 👨‍👩‍👧 Patient Demographics

Largest patient group:

**30–39 Years**

Gender distribution remains nearly balanced.

---

### 📊 Admissions

Admissions and non-admissions are almost equally distributed, indicating a balanced mix of critical and non-critical ER cases.
